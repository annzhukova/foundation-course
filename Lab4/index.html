About me

Hi, my name is Anna Zhukova. I live in Tallinn. I am a bachelor and a master of Applied Mathematics and Informatics. I studied Statistics, Algorithms, and different methods for creating and evaluating maths models.

My bachelor's degree thesis was about superconductivity in graphene nanochannel structures. I had studied eigenfunctions and eigenvalues of a particle in a complex chain and built a mathematical model for researching properties of superconductivity. I used Green's function and theory of zero radius potential for approximating resolving this task.

I worked as a frontend engineer at Yandex in 2010-2012 years where I got a bit of experience working with user interfaces and usability.

In 2012 I founded a startup Sportfolio. For me it was a great life learning and experience.

During 2014-2024 I was on maternity leave.

In March 2020 I took a course of Applied Data Science Specialization in Coursera.org. It was based on Python language and visualisation tools. I was finishing it when I was waiting for the birth of my child.

In 2025 I started my HCI Master Program.

My hobby are sport, travel, bicycle travel with family, ecology, baking printed gingerbread (http://maguslinn.com), videography, blogging.

My projects

Sportfolio

2011 - 2015

Sportfolio was providing a service to managers of sport events to organize their competitions and for athletes to participate in them and to record their achievements. We were the first to provide such a service in Russia. The biggest contest for Sportfolio was the ERGO White nights marathon (annual marathon in Saint Petersburg, Russia) in 2014. There were 6k participants from 40 countries and our service helped a lot with preparing data and accepting payments as well as providing insurance services via online registration form. About 50% participants had registered online, thus saving time and money for both categories of our customers: organizers and athletes.
